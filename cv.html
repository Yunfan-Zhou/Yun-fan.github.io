Research profile. I am a jointly trained Ph.D. student at Hangzhou Dianzi University and the National Astronomical Observatories, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My current research interests are radio astronomy and galaxy cosmology, with experience in radio spectral-line data, astronomical data processing pipelines, and observational searches for faint physical signals.

Research Interests

Radio astronomy; H I observations; galaxy cosmology; low-surface- brightness and ultradiffuse galaxies; axion dark matter searches; astronomical big-data processing and visualization.

Research and Work Experience

  • 2022.07 – 2023.12 Zhejiang Lab — Research Assistant, Astronomical Big Data Intelligent Computing and Service Platform
    Contributed to the FAST CRAFTS wide-band data-processing pipeline, including standing-wave removal, off-source background estimation, baseline removal, and pipeline testing.
    CRAFTS data portal: https://hiverse.zero2x.org/wide
  • 2021.04 – 2022.11 Axion dark matter search with MeerKAT — First-author research project
    Processed and analysed MeerKAT UHF-band observations of the neutron star J0806.4−4123 to search for narrow radio signatures of axion-photon conversion and constrain axion-photon coupling.
  • 2020.09 – 2022.10 H I search for ultradiffuse galaxies — First-author research project
    Used HIPASS data from the Parkes telescope to search for neutral- hydrogen counterparts of DES low-surface-brightness and ultradiffuse galaxy candidates.
